People are not just ones or zeros.

Inclusive design is...

Being mindful of gender differences.

Start with Why

  • Why does my site care about gender?
  • Why does my site need gender pronouns?
  • why not tell users?

Move to How

  • How to design for other genders?
  • How to be gender neutral?
  • How to give users choices?

Rebel!

  • Don't wait for priority/permission.
  • Be innovative with approaches.
  • At least add an "other" option.
